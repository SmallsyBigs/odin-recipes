Lasagna

Dish description:

Lasagna is a dish that needs no introduction. An abolute Italian classic that will make your friends go wow!
It is not the easiest recipe in the world, but the mouth-watering taste is so worth it.
Get your chef hat ready and let's go!

Ingridient list:

  • Ragu sauce
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mozzarella
  • Provolone cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Beschamel sauce

Steps

  1. Soak the sheets in water for 10 minutes.
  2. Prepare your sauces and shred cheeses.
  3. In an oven dish layer your ingridients:
    • A little ragu on the base of the dish
    • Lasania sheets
    • More ragu
    • Cheeses
    • Beschamel souce
    • Repet until desied height of the dish
    • Finish with final sheets and lots of cheese
  4. Cover the dish and put in an oven at 180°C for 35 minutes.
  5. Take the lasagna of and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
